Today was Jungfroujock day. We travelled to "The top of Europe" by catching the train from Interlaken to Grindelwald, then the ski Gondola up to Eigergletcher, then the cogwheel train up to Jungfraujoch. 

We did the discovery loop which included an elevator up to the observation deck (-10 degrees outside), then the ice palace (ice cave with ice carvings), then outside to view the mountains.

It took a while to get used to the altitude, but we were rewarded with some incredible views and landscape. On the way down we hoped out at Eigergletcher to walk on the snow, take some photos, and watch some skiers head off before making our descent back to Interlaken.

Ayla note: Ayla loved the "ice blocks" (walls of the ice palace) and tickled them ("tickle tickle").
